The ArcRise Way

Your Habits Are Chapters, Not Chores

ArcRise treats every habit as an arc in your story. Each Clear earns EXP. Each streak builds momentum. Each level brings you closer to mastery.

Track Habits as Arcs

Stop logging tasks. Start writing arcs. Set clear daily, weekly, or monthly goals that mean something, then complete them.

Earn EXP & Level Up

Every Clear you earn feeds into a calibrated EXP system. Watch effort compound — discipline becomes visible progress.

Ascend Tiers & Build Legacy

Rise from Novice to Master. Each tier is proof of consistency, not luck. Your arc history is the legacy you're building.

Features

Forged for Focus

Everything you need to build lasting habits — nothing you don't.

Habit Arcs

Daily, weekly, and monthly arcs with flexible goals. Customise difficulty and let EXP rewards scale accordingly.

EXP Progression

Harder habits pay more EXP, simpler ones pay less. Pick the difficulty honestly and the numbers will reflect it.

Hero Tiers

Novice through Master — five tiers that open when your EXP hits the threshold. You can't buy your way up and you can't skip ahead.

Streak Momentum

Streaks built to motivate. Hero Streak multipliers stack your EXP gains the longer you stay consistent.

Home Screen Widgets

Six widget types — from a quick Clear counter to an interactive arc list. Your progress always visible, always motivating.

Arc Journeys

A lifetime history view for every arc you've ever run. See when you started, when you broke through, where you stand.

Smart Reminders

Per-arc reminders that learn your best time. Set it once, and ArcRise finds the moment you're most likely to follow through.

Achievement Awards

Earn milestone awards across streaks, mastery, and Clears. A living shelf that reflects the person you're becoming.

HealthKit Sync

Link arcs to your steps, sleep, and workouts. ArcRise reads from Health and marks your arc cleared automatically.
